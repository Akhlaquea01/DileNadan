
14.	Bahut Dard Hai Ai Jaan-e-Adaa Teri Mohabbat Mein,
Kaise Keh Doon Ki Tujhe Wafa Nibhani Nahi Aati.
15.	Kitna Lutf Le Rahein Hain Log Mere Dard-o-Gham Ka,
Ai Ishq Dekh Tu Ne To Mera Tamaasha Hi Bana Diya.
16.	Tera Pyar Paane Ke Liye
Maine Kitna Intezar Kiya,
Aur Uss Intezar Mein Na Jaane
Kitno Se Pyar Kiya.
17.	Tera Khayal Dil Se Mitaya Nahi Abhi,
BeWafa Maine Tujhko Bhulaya Nahi Abhi.
18.	Humse Na Kariye Baatein Yoon Berukhi Se Sanam,
Hone Lage Ho Kuchh-Kuchh Bewafa Se Tum.
19.	Fariyad Kar Rahi Hai Ye Tarsi Huyi Nigaah,
Dekhe Huye Kisi Ko Zamana Gujar Gaya.
20.	Ye Keh-Keh Ke Hum Dil Ko Samjha Rahe Hain,
Wo Ab Chal Chuke Hai Wo Ab Aa Rahe Hain.
21.	Raah Takte Huye Jab Thak Gayi Meri Aankhein,
Fir Tujhe Dhoondne Meri Aankh Ke Aansoo Nikle.
22.	Mujhe Maloom Hai Tumne Bahut Barsaat Dekhi Hai,
Magar Meri Inhi Aankhon Se Sawan Haar Jaata Hai.
23.	Hum Unki Har Khwahish
Poori Karne Ka Vaada Kar Baithhe,
Humein Kya Pata Tha
Humein Chhodna Hi Unki Khwahish Hogi.
24.	Bahut Khaas The Kabhi
Najron Mein Kisi Ke Hum Bhi,
Magar Najaron Ke Takaaze
Badalne Me Der Kahan Lagti Hai.
25.	Manta Hi Nahi Kambakht Dil Usey Chahne Se,
Main Haath Jodta Hoon To Ye Gale Pad Jata Hai.
26.	Na Poochh Dil Ki Hakiqat Magar Ye Kehta Hai,
Wo Bhi Bekaraar Rahe Jisne Bekarar Kiya.
27.	Mujhe Kuchh Bhi Nahi Kehna Itni Si Gujarish Hai,
Bas Utni Baar Mil Jaao Ki Jitna Yaad Aate Ho.
28.	Har Ek Pehlu Tera Mere Dil Me Aabaad Ho Jaye,
Tujhe Main Iss Kadar Dekhu Mujhe Tu Yaad Ho Jaye.
29.	Ye Sach Hai Ki Hum Mohabbat Se Darte Hain,
Kyun Ki Ye Pyar Dil Ko Bahut Tadpata Hai,
Aankh Mein Aansoo To Hum Chhupa Sakte Hain,
Dard-e-Dil Duniya Ko Pata Chal Jata Hai.
30.	Main Ek Shaam Jo Roshan Deeya Uthha Laya,
Tamaam Shahar Kahin Se Hawa Uthha Laya.
31.	Najron Mein Doston Ki Jo Itna Kharab Hai,
Uska Qasoor Ye Hai Ki Wo Kaamyab Hai.
32.	Wo Taaro Ki Tarah Raat Bhar Chamkte Rahe,
Hum Chaand Se Tanha Safar Karte Rahe,
Wo To Beete Waqt The Unhein Aana Na Tha,
Hum Yoon Hi Saari Raat Karbat Badalte Rahe.
33.	Zindagi KashmKash-e-Ishq Ke Aagaz Ka Naam,
Maut Anjaam Hai Isee Dard Ke Afsaane Ka.
34.	हर वक़्त का हँसना तुझे बर्बाद ना कर दे,
तन्हाई के लम्हों में कभी रो भी लिया कर।
Har Waqt Ka Hansna Tujhe Barbaad Na Kar De,
Tanhai Ke Lamhon Mein Kabhi Ro Bhi Liya Kar.
35.	Tanhayian Kuchh Iss Tarah Se Dasne Lagi Mujhe,
Main Aaj Apne Pairon Ki Aahat Se Darr Gaya.
36.	Kabhi Lafz Bhool Jaaun Kabhi Baat Bhool Jaaun,
Tujhe Iss Kadar Chahun Ki Apni Jaat Bhool Jaaun,
Kabhi Uthh Ke Tere Paas Se Jo Main Chal Dun,
Jaate Huye Khud Ko Tere Paas Bhool Jaaun.
37.	Wo Tere Khat Teri Tasvir Aur Sookhe Phool,
Bahut Udaas Karti Hain Mujhko Nishaniyan Teri.
38.	Ab Na Kholo Mere Ghar Ke Udaas Darwaze,
Hawa Ka Shor Meri Uljhanein Bada Deta Hai.
39.	Mere Dushman Bhi Mere Mureed Hain Shayad,
Waqt-BeWaqt Mera Naam Liya Karte Hain,
Meri Gali Se Gujarte Hain Chhupa Ke Khanzar,
Ru-Ba-Ru Hone Par Salaam Kiya Karte Hain.
40.	Haalat Ke Kadamo Par Sikandar Nahi Jhukte,
Toote Huye Taare Kabhi Zamin Par Nahi Girte,
Bade Shauk Se Girti Hai Lahrein Samundar Mein,
Par Samundar Kabhi Lahron Mein Nahi Girte.
41.	Hausle Bhi Kisi Hakeem Se Kam Nahi Hote,
Har Takleef Mein Takat Ki Davaa Dete Hain.
42.	Ek Sooraj Tha Ki Taaro Ke Gharane Se Uthha,
Aankh Hairan Hai Kya Shakhs Zamane Se Uthha.
43.	Hamein Na Mohabbat Mili Na Pyar Mila,
Humko Jo Bhi Mila Bewafa Yaar Mila,
Apni To Ban Gayi Tamasha Zindagi,
Har Koyi Maksad Ka Talabgaar Mila.
44.	Shikayat Kya Karoon Dono Taraf Gham Ka Fasaana Hai,
Mere Aage Mohobbat Hai Tere Aage Zamana Hai.
45.	Dekh Kar Tumko Aksar Humein Ye Ehsaas Hota Hai,
Kabhi Kabhi Gham Dene Wala Bhi Kitna Khaas Hota Hai.
46.	Hum Chaahe To Bhi Tujhe Bhula Nahi Sakte,
Teri Yaadon Se Daaman Chura Nahi Sakte,
Tere Bina Jeena Ek Pal Bhi Mumkin Nahi,
Tumhein Chahte Hain Itna Ki Bata Nahi Sakte.

Adalat Bhi Teri Thi Gawaah Bhi Tu Hi Thi.
48.	Seekh Kar Gaya Hai Wo Mohabbat Mujhse,
Jis Se Bhi Karega Be-Misaal Karega.
49.	Chalte-Chalte Akele Ab Thak Gaye Hum,
Jo Manzil Ko Jaye Wo Dagar Chahiye.
Tanhayi Ka Bojh Ab Aur UthTa Nahi,
Ab Humko Bhi Ek HumSafar Chahiye,
50.	Bade Hi Ajeeb Hain Ye Zindagi Ke Raaste,
Anjane Mod Par Kuchh Log Apne Ban Jate Hain,
Milne Ki Khushi Dein Ya Na Dein,
Magar Bichadne Ka Gam Zaroor De Jate Hain.
51.	Kash Yeh Zalim Judai Na Hoti,
Ai Khuda Tu Ne Yeh Cheej Banayi Na Hoti,
Na Hum Unse Milte Na Pyar Hota,
Apni Zindagi Fir Parayi Na Hoti.
52.	Sitam To Ye Hai Ke Zalim Sukhan-Sanaas Nahin,
Wo Ek Shakhs Jo Shayar Banaa Gaya Mujhko.
53.	Sher-o-Sukhan Kya Koi Bachchon Ka Khel Hai?
Jal Jaati Hain Jawaniyan Lafzon Ki Aag Mein.
54.	Iss Dil Ki Daastaan Bhi Badi Ajeeb Hoti Hai,
Badi Mushkil Se Ise Khushi Naseeb Hoti Hai,
Kisi Ke Paas Aane Par Khushi Ho Na Ho,
Par Door Jaane Par Badi Takleef Hoti Hai.
55.	Kahin Ye Apni Mohabbat Ki Intehaan To Nahi,
Bahut Dino Se Teri Yaad Bhi Nahi Aayi.
56.	Kaash Tu Bhi Ban Jaye Teri Yaadon Ki Tarah,
Na Waqt Dekhe Na Bahana, Bas Chali Aaye.
